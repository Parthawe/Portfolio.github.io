Overview

Developing OTT Platform for connoisseurs and professionals for opera, ballet, symphonies, and classical music.

Summary CueTV is an OTT platform for connoisseurs and professionals for opera, ballet, symphonies, and classical music. Developed in collaboration with MonsoonFish, the platform provides a seamless streaming experience across all devices — computers, tablets, smartphones, and smart TVs — bringing world-class performing arts to audiences globally.
The Challenges The main challenge was to figure out the target audience and tailor the OTT Platform and the Retargeting Ads for the User Audience.
My Role As a UI Designer & Research, my role was to get to identify the audience and Create Awareness by helping them engage by using Google, Facebook Ads & giving a smooth experience on the OTT Platform for easy movement from Home to the Opera show they want to watch. Also watch across all screens in HD: computers, tablets, smartphones, and smart TVs.

Identified Use Cases

We mapped content along multiple dimensions to understand the full scope of the platform:

  • By payment: Free titles, pay-per-view, subscription
  • By categories: Video on demand, livestreams, premieres, master class, entire festivals, competitions
  • By genres: Opera, ballet, classical, drama, documentary
  • By content within: Single title, multiple segments, stand-alone performances, multiple days/events, multiple languages
  • By duplicates: Same title with different companies, same title with same company but different year
  • By recognition of title: Award winners, blockbusters, new or CueTV-featured, highly recognized artists/companies, regular

Strategy

Focusing on Awareness

Aim: to understand if collaborating with magazines helps us with awareness.

Experiment 2

Aim: to confirm the set 3 user segment, and see how they react to free trial campaign.

Experiment 3

Aim: to experiment where do the users land after targeting ads and evaluate the response.

Plan of Action

Awareness Discovery Validation Targeting Yay! We got a Sign Up

The plan followed a four-step loop: Awareness → Discovery → Validation → Targeting, ultimately leading to sign-ups.

Research

Customer Segments

We identified three distinct customer segments, each with different motivations, pain points, and channels to reach them:

Set 1 — Casual Enthusiasts
Always been able to afford conventional theatre. Inspired by the experience, socially enriched taste. Values favourites, shows at their fingertips across all screens. Reached through luxury travel, tourism, news, and exclusive printed magazines.
Set 2 — Dedicated Patrons
Civically engaged to classical art, up to date on conventional theatres. Inspired by appreciation of art and the finer details. Values range of collections and new shows. Reached through art & music newsletters and forums.
Set 3 — Students & Learners
Learning, studying the musicality of art. Inspired by the study of art itself. Values accessibility, free trials, and the vast library. Reached through social media, educational institutions, and instrument apps.
Types of Ads
Google Ads (responsive, image, video, app promotion), Facebook Ads (carousel, image, instant experience, video, collection), and other networks including Criteo, Skim Links, Out Brain, and AppNexus.

Output

30,000+ Different Ads

We produced over 30,000 different ad creatives tailored to each customer segment and platform, covering shows like Romeo and Juliet, Hercules, Carmen, Der Zwerg, Cinderella, Macbeth, Fortunio, Agrippina, Jake Arditti, and many more.

Engagement Results

The before-and-after comparison showed a significant uplift in both trialist views and subscriber views after deploying the targeted ad campaigns, with a marked spike in engagement visible from August to September 2021.

Product Design

Platform Optimization

Beyond the ad system, we optimized the CueTV platform experience itself across all touchpoints.

Watch
Live Streams And Premieres
The explore tab surfaces recorded streams, live performances, summer festivals, and premieres from opera houses worldwide — including titles like Siberia, Nerone, La Traviata, and Moise et Pharaon.
Find
Musical Works
A powerful search experience lets users find specific works like Macbeth with preview clips, full performances, and related works like Lady Macbeth of Mtensk.
Find
Composers
Search by composer name like Mozart to discover their full catalogue of available performances and recordings.
Find
Companies & Festivals
Search by opera house or festival — such as the Royal Opera House — to browse collections including Giselle, The Winter's Tale, Madama Butterfly, The Nutcracker, The Sleeping Beauty, and Alice's Adventures in Wonderland.
Save And
Watch Offline
Users can download performances to watch offline, making the platform accessible even without an internet connection.
