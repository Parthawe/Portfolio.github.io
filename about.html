Founding Product Designer & Artist

Parth Pawar is a multidisciplinary Product Designer who merges creativity with technology to craft immersive, user-centered experiences. His work spans UI/UX design, interactive installations, and digital fabrication, bridging the gap between digital systems and physical engagement.

He has led impactful projects ranging from Asia's Web3 crypto transaction system to dynamic stage designs for live performance, showcasing a unique ability to integrate technical precision with creative vision. Alongside his design work, Parth contributes to education as a Teaching Assistant at NYU, supporting courses like "Art as Interactive Medium" and "100 Days of Making."

With a Master's from NYU and a Bachelor's in Computer Science, he brings both academic depth and hands-on expertise to every project.
