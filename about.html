About me

Designer, builder,
creative technologist

25-year-old designer driven by curiosity, systems thinking, and the joy of solving real problems through thoughtful digital experiences.

I design human centered products across AI wearables, fintech, and interactive systems

Currently I am the Head of UI/UX at Mentra, where I lead the design of AI-powered smart glasses — the only wearable with an open-source OS and a miniapp store. I design the companion app, MentraOS interfaces, and the developer platform that makes smart glasses extensible. Previously I was the Founding Product Designer at ZentiPay, building a fintech super app from 0 to 1, and Lead Product Designer at TransFi, Asia's leading crypto payments platform.

I hold a Master's from NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program at Tisch School of the Arts, where I studied at the intersection of design, technology, and human interaction. Before that, I earned a Bachelor's in Computer Science from Vishwakarma Institute of Technology and completed an AI research internship at IBM focused on cancer prognosis interfaces.

My work bridges product design and creative technology. I build interactive installations, design typefaces, and create performance software alongside my product work. This cross disciplinary practice gives me a unique lens on how people interact with systems, screens, and physical spaces.

AI wearables, smart glasses UX, fintech product design, AI interface design, design systems, user research, interactive installations, creative technology, cross cultural UX
