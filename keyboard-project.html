Custom Keyboard

Mechanical keyboard as interactive art installation

Year2023
RoleBuilder & Designer
ToolsKiCad, QMK Firmware, Hand Soldering

Overview

A hand-soldered 65% mechanical keyboard built as an interactive sculpture. Every component was chosen for aesthetic and tactile intentionality — brass weight, lubed switches, artisan keycaps — exploring the intersection of craft and daily use tools.
