Jugalbandi

AI music collaboration platform

Year2024
RoleProduct Designer
ToolsFigma, TouchDesigner, User Research

Overview

Jugalbandi (meaning 'a duet' in Hindi) is an AI music tool that lets musicians improvise with generative AI in real-time. I designed the session interface and the visual language for representing AI-generated musical phrases.
