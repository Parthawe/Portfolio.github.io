Raahi

Integrated public transport navigation for Pune

Year2023
RoleUX Designer
ToolsFigma, Principle, User Interviews

Overview

Raahi connects buses, metro, auto-rickshaws and shared cabs into one seamless navigation experience. Research with 60+ commuters uncovered pain points around multi-modal transfers, last-mile connectivity and real-time information gaps.
