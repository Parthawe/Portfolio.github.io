Redefining AI Voice Selection For Enterprise

Integrating Emotional Intelligence and Expressive Voice Selection: A Reimagined AI Voice Experience

ClientVoice AI (NDA)
ScopeProduct Design
RoleProduct Designer
Duration3 Months
Year2025
Contents Research Define Concept A Concept B Voice DNA Mood Matching Validate

Reimagining how enterprise teams discover and select AI voices.

Summary The reimagined AI voice matching experience helps enterprise teams and creatives select AI voices through intuitive, emotionally intelligent interactions. Instead of static dropdowns, users explore, test, and build voices through personalized, context-aware systems. This approach balances technical power with playful creativity and emotional alignment.
The Challenges Bridging the gap between enterprise-grade AI voice tools and intuitive, emotion-driven UX. Replacing dropdown-based selection with expressive, guided exploration. Supporting both fast onboarding and deep customization for varied user needs.
My Role Product Design Strategy: Leading concept definition for how users engage with AI voice personalities.
Interaction & System Design: Designing DNA-based and mood-based systems for selecting and shaping voice outputs.
Tools & Techniques Figma AI User Research & Testing Emotion-Centric UX Design Voice Prototyping User Flows User Study Workflow Optimization
Research

The voices are world-class. The discovery experience isn't.

AI's voices are human-like, expressive, and built for real-world business applications — but the way customers discover and choose them doesn't do them justice.

Design Process

Empathize Define Ideate Prototype Test

Comparative UX analysis across three platforms (Rime, Central, Simple) revealed critical gaps in Entry Point design, First-Time Flow, Voice Discovery UX, Customization & Personality Fit, and Onboarding Support — confirming the need for a fundamentally new approach to voice selection.

Define

How might we help Enterprise Teams select an expressive AI voice that aligns with brand tone?

Pain Points

Unintuitive
Current voice selection relies on static lists and dropdowns that don't convey personality or tone.
Uncontextualized
Voices are presented without context for how they sound in real-world use cases or brand scenarios.
Fragmented
The selection process is scattered across multiple screens and lacks a cohesive flow.
Impersonal
No emotional connection or personality fit between the user's needs and the voice options.
Rigid
Minimal customization options and no ability to shape or tweak voices to match brand identity.

Customer Journey Map

Mapped the Enterprise Manager journey across five phases:

1
Awareness
Enterprise manager discovers AI voice platform and begins evaluating options for their brand.
2
Exploration
Browses available voices, listens to samples, and tries to match voices to brand requirements.
3
Evaluation
Shortlists voices, tests them in context, and compares options against brand tone guidelines.
4
Selection
Finalizes voice choice, customizes parameters, and confirms the voice for implementation.
5
Handoff to Tech
Exports voice configuration and hands off to engineering team for integration.
Ideate — Concept A

Voice DNA Builder

Instead of picking a name from a list, users "build" a voice by choosing parameters that form into a helix: Emotion, Accent, Pace, and Texture. The system animates a DNA strand building in real time, and a voice is generated from it.

Layout Architecture

Left Panel
Navigation and voice persona selection panel for quick access to saved configurations.
Center Stage
DNA strand visualization with parameter sliders for Emotion, Accent, Pace, and Texture.
Right Panel
Transcript display and voice preview controls for real-time testing of configurations.
Bottom Right
Interactive global map for picking regional accents with cultural context.
Ideate — Concept B

Mood-Based Voice Matching

User speaks a sentence, the AI analyzes tone, pacing, and emotion, then suggests three voices that match or balance their energy. The system adapts to the user, not the other way around.

Three Stages

1
User Speaks
User delivers an initial speech prompt. The silhouette pulses in response to vocal input.
2
Voice-Matching Logic
AI processes emotion, pacing, and tone from the speech to identify matching voice profiles.
3
Persona Suggestions
Dynamic voice personas appear with mood-match summaries, showing how each voice aligns with the user's energy.

Deep Dive — Mood-Based Voice Matching

The system adapts to you.

User speaks a sentence. AI analyzes tone, pacing, and emotion, then suggests three voices that match or balance their energy. It flips the script: the system adapts to the user, not the other way around. Responsive onboarding based on emotion.

Three Screens

1
Listening Mode
"Speak your mind" — a glowing circle pulses as it captures the user's voice input.
2
Thinking Mode
A silhouette appears as the AI processes emotion and generates matching voice profiles.
3
Talking Mode
Three voice suggestions appear with a red/orange gradient, alongside transcript details and mood-match summaries.

Validate

A/B Testing: DNA Builder vs. Mood Matching

Tested Prototype A (Voice DNA Builder) against Prototype B (Mood-Based Voice Matching) with 7 participants via WhatsApp.

Results

Ease of Onboarding
Winner: Mood Matching (B) — 5 out of 7 participants preferred its voice-first, low-friction entry.
Personalization & Control
Winner: DNA Builder (A) — 6 out of 7 participants valued the deep customization options.
Visual Engagement
Winner: DNA Builder (A) — 5 out of 7 found the DNA strand animation more visually compelling.
Emotional Connection
Winner: Mood Matching (B) — 4 out of 7 felt stronger emotional resonance with voice-first input.
Clarity of Steps
Winner: Mood Matching (B) — 4 out of 7 found the three-stage flow easier to follow.
Fun & Playfulness
Winner: DNA Builder (A) — 6 out of 7 enjoyed the creative, exploratory interactions.

Overall Winner: Voice DNA Builder (Prototype A) for its customization depth and creative freedom. Recommendation: Use Mood-Based Matching as an emotional onboarding layer, then let users graduate to Voice DNA Builder for full control.

Reflections

Working on the AI voice matching platform gave me the opportunity to deeply explore how voice can evolve from a utility into an expressive interface for emotion, identity, and brand storytelling.
