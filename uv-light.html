UV Light Installation

Invisible spectra made visible

Year2022
RoleInstallation Artist
ToolsUV LEDs, Arduino, Theatrical Lighting

Overview

A site-specific installation using UV fluorescent paint and reactive materials to reveal hidden layers of a space. Viewers move through zones of visible and invisible light, exploring the perceptual limits of human vision.
