ZentiPay

Fintech super-app for seamless international transfers

Year2025
RoleLead UX Designer
ToolsFigma, Maze, Hotjar

Overview

ZentiPay is a fintech super-app designed for migrant workers and international students to send money home with zero friction. The design system prioritized trust, clarity and speed — reducing transfer time perception by 40% in usability tests.
